Daily Dua

All Praise is due to Almighty اللّـه Ta'ala , The Forgiver and Concealer of Faults, The Guide to Repentance, The ONE who Responds to Du'aas

Salaat, Salaam and Salutations Upon The Guide of The Believers, Sayyedena Rasulullah  صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ

اللهم كن لنا واجعلنا لك
"O Allah! Be for us and make us for YOU"

رَبِّ إِنِّي لِمَا أَنْزَلْتَ إِلَيَّ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَقِيرٌ
"My Lord, indeed I am, in need for whatever good You would send down to me"

لَّآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّآ أَنتَ سُبْحَٰنَكَ إِنِّى كُنتُ مِنَ ٱلظَّٰلِمِينَ
None has the right to be worshipped except You; Glory be unto You. Indeed I have been from the wrongdoers.

O OUR MOST COMPASSIONATE MOST CHERISHED MOST BELOVED MOST MERCIFUL MOST GIVING MOST FORGIVING اللّـه, we repent before YOU from all our sins and promise O اللّـه , never to return to it ever (with YOUR help ). YOUR forgiveness is far more extensive than our sins and YOUR Mercy, O اللّـه , is far more promising  to us than our good actions. Forgive us, our parents, teachers, brothers, sisters, children, family, friends, and the Ummah of our beloved Nabi  صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّم. Bless us with Istiqaamah (steadfastness) Ikhlaas (sincerity) Qubuliyyah (acceptance) Set right our deen (religious) life which is a safeguard in all our affairs. And set right our worldly life which is a source of our earning and maintenance. And set right our life of the hereafter to which is our ultimate return. And O اللّـه , keep us alive so long as it is in our best interest and take us from this world when death is in our best interest.
O اللّـه , bless us, forgive us, protect us, and keep us steadfast on Siraatul Mustaqeem, and accept this du'aa of ours,  آمِيْن يَارَبَّ الْعَالَمِينْ

صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ
صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ
صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ

Subhaanarabbika Rabbil 'Izzati 'Amma Yassifoon Wassalaamun 'Alal Mursaleen Walhumdulillahir Rabbil 'Aalameen

Reminder read the following dua 3 times after Fajr Salaah إن شاء الله 

سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ: 
عَدَدَ خَلْقِهِ، 
وَرِضَا نَفْسِهِ، 
وَزِنَةَ عَرْشِهِ 
وَمِدَادَ كَلِمَاتِهِ

Subhan-ALLAHI wa bihamdihi, `adada khalqihi, wa rida nafsihi, wa zinatah `arshihi, wa midada kalimatihi

Glory is to Allah and praise is to Him, by the multitude of His creation, by His Pleasure, by the weight of His Throne, and by the extent of His Words.
